About

This website is a collection of links intended to introduce new comrades to Marxism-Leninism. These resources, by all means, are not the only works one should engage with in their journey of becoming a Marxist-Leninist, nor are they the only way to becoming a Marxist-Leninist. Consider this website as a tool to begin your process of unlearning and learning. If you have any questions or suggestions, feel free to contact me on Instagram!

The Principles of Communism

The Principles of Communism is a short pamphlet written by Frederick Engels that provides new communists with definitions and explanations on concepts fundamental to Marxism. I consider this to be an absolutely necessary work to read in order to begin learning communist ideals; not only is it short, but it breaks down convoluted vocabulary!

Socialism: Utopian and Scientific

Socialism: Utopian and Scientific is yet another pamphlet authored by Engels. Throughout this work, Engels details several utopian socialisms and their shortcomings, the signficance of dialectics to Marxism, and historical materialism.
